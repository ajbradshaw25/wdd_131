About Us

Here at Sapphire Creations, I make all kinds of baked goods, desserts, and confections. Baking has been a hobby of mine for years. I use this skill I've learned daily. Usually, I make pastries and desserts for my friends, neighbors, and family. When I make these dishes for them, I am just glad to hear how much they like my creations. My purpose here on this site is to provide you with the knowledge that I gained from my own experiences. I will do this through online classes here at Sapphire Creations.

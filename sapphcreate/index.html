About Us

Here at Sapphire Creations, I make all kinds of baked goods, desserts, and confections. Baking has been a hobby of mine for years. I use this skill that I've learned daily. Usually, I make pastries and desserts for my friends, neighbors, and family. When I make these dishes for them, I am just glad to hear how much they like my creations. My purpose here on this site is to provide you with the knowledge that I gained from my own experiences. I will do this through online classes here at Sapphire Creations.

to learn a couple of tips and tricks that will help you or your children in your everyday lives of cooking and baking. And finally, you can expect to have lots of fun while learning and actually baking along with me!
